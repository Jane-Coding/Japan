日本

The name for Japan in Japanese is written using the kanji 日本 and is pronounced Nippon or Nihon.The characters 日本 mean "sun origin", which is the source of the popular Western epithet "Land of the Rising Sun".

Emoji

The word emoji comes from Japanese e 絵 meaning picture and moji, 文字 meaning character. Emojis developed as a solution to miscommunication problems that arose from the increasing use of emails.

The cherry blossom is known as sakura 桜 in Japanese and symbolises not only the return of spring but of renewal and hope. The cherry blossom is Japan's unofficial national flower and it appears on the 100 Yen coin.

折る oru - to fold and 紙 kami - paper. When origami was first practiced it was a craft only for the elite due to the high price of the paper. Originally it was used for religious purposes.

数 suu - number, 独 doku single. Sudoku is a logic-based number-placement puzzle. It is a registered trademark in Japan, and the puzzle is generally referred to as Number Place.
